About The JARs

The JARs is a small family-driven studio that combines data skills, design thinking and content ideas in one place. We work remotely with clients who need reliable support instead of a big agency.

From training AI models and cleaning spreadsheets to designing logos and planning social media content, we keep everything simple, practical and easy to use.

What we can help with

  • Data annotation & AI training: Clean, well-labeled datasets for computer vision or NLP projects.
  • Excel & SQL: Automated reports, dashboards and queries so you always know what is happening in your numbers.
  • Branding & design: Logos, basic brand kits and kid-influencer profiles that look consistent across platforms.
  • Social content: Reels ideas, captions and simple editing so you can post faster without overthinking.
  • Creative assistance: Research, presentations and custom projects when you need an extra brain on your side.
